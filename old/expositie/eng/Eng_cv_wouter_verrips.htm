CV Wouter Verrips

The artist Wouter Verrips studied sculpture and painting at Rijksakademie van Beeldende Kunsten Amsterdam and Stage design at 'Hochschule der Kunste' Berlin-West.
Since 1981 he runs his own studio in Amsterdam, Holland.
Since 1993 he divides his time between his Amsterdam studio and his studio in France.
and works in other countries such as Spain and the USA.

1983-1998     was head and teacher of the fine art department of the culture organization of Amsterdam University.
1993 - now    runs and teaches a summer school for the arts in France. 

He create drawings, paintings, sculptures, WebPages and graphics. 
He was awarded Prix Public at Prix des Volcans in Clermont-Ferrand.
He regularly shows his work in France, Holland and other countries.
A list of exhibits is available on this link.
His works are present in the collections of the Dutch government and various private collections from Moscow to Los Angeles.
